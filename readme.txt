[Metricks](https://www.metricks.io/) is a powerful tool allowing you to automate customer affiliate programs for your WooCommerce store.

Metricks helps you increase your sales by rewarding your customers when they refer people to your store. We give your customers a personal affiliate link that they can share with their friends.
